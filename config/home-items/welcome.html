Our ultimate aim of this module is to provide reporting and help you make better promotional planning decisions with a single, intuitive tool.

This brand new module is being developed using data science and contains the most advanced algorithms Acme have ever built to analyse the financial and customer impacts of promotions.

The focus of this first development stage is to give you a comprehensive view of how your promotions have performed so that you can learn more from past promotions including:

  • Financial metrics to show you how a promotion performed, and what the true, net impact of the promotion was on the category.
  • Customer metrics to show you who bought a promotion and how it changed their behaviour, helping you to target your promotional plans to the right customers.
